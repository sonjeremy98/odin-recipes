Korean Ramen (Ramyun)

Description

Making korean ramen (also known as ramyun) is very simple. It involves using the ingredients that come in the package, as well as some other ingredients, such as green onions or eggs.

Ingredients

  • Ramen noodles
  • Packaged veggies
  • Seasoning packet
  • Green onions
  • Eggs
  • Water

Steps

  1. Pour water into a pot, leave on high heat
  2. Pour seasoning packet into pot while water is warm
  3. Once soup is boiling, pour packaged veggies and ramen noodles into pot
  4. Put eggs and green onions in roughly 10 seconds after previous step
  5. Stir the pot for about 3-4 minutes
  6. EAT UP
